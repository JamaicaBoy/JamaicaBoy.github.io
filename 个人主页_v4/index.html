关于我About

我的研究围绕制造业的一个共性痛点：怎样少花实验、少出废品，又快又准地把产品做好。围绕这个目标我做了三件事——让工厂在开机生产前就能预测成品质量、用很少几次试验自动优化工艺参数、以及用大模型辅助工艺调参。这些方法都做到了能在仿真和真实产线上跑通、能开源、能写成论文。我希望在企业研发岗位继续把前沿方法变成真正可落地的产品价值。 My research targets a pain point shared across manufacturing: how to make good products quickly and accurately while spending fewer experiments and producing less scrap. Toward this I have done three things—predicting product quality before production starts, automatically optimizing process parameters with very few trials, and using LLMs to assist process tuning. Each method runs on both simulation and real production lines, is open-sourced, and is published. I want to keep turning frontier methods into real, deployable product value in an industrial R&D role.

生产前产品质量智能预测Pre-production Product Quality Prediction

  • 解决的问题：在一批产品正式生产之前，仅凭设定好的工艺参数就提前预测成品质量是否达标，帮助工厂在开机前筛掉有风险的参数，减少废品和反复试模。The problem: before a batch goes into production, predict whether the product will meet quality targets using only the planned process settings—so the factory can screen out risky parameters before startup, cutting scrap and repeated trial molding.
  • 做法：把工厂以往的大量生产数据整理成一个可检索的"经验库"，让当前的参数设置自动对照最相似的历史批次，从而给出可靠的质量预测。The approach: turn the factory's large body of past production data into a searchable "experience base," so the current settings are automatically matched against the most similar historical batches to yield a reliable prediction.
  • 效果：在真实生产数据上验证，质量预测误差比现有最好的方法最多降低 36%，更换材料后依然适用——这是我目前最有代表性的工作。The result: on real production data, prediction error is up to 36% lower than the best existing method and still holds when switching materials—my most representative work to date.

少样本工艺参数智能优化Few-shot Process Parameter Optimization

  • 解决的问题：高端制造里工艺参数多、试验贵，多个质量指标还常常互相冲突，靠人工试模又慢又不稳定。The problem: in high-end manufacturing there are many parameters, experiments are expensive, quality targets often conflict, and manual trial molding is slow and unstable.
  • 做法：用很少几次试验加上历史数据，自动判断每个参数该往哪个方向调、调多少，不需要复杂的物理模型，也不需要大量训练数据。The approach: with just a few trials plus historical data, automatically decide which direction and how much to tune each parameter—no complex physics model and no large training set required.
  • 效果：在仿真和真实注塑机上验证，通常 7–9 次调整就能稳定达到目标，受到突发干扰后 1–2 次即可恢复，整体效果比常用方法提升 33% 以上。The result: validated in simulation and on real molding machines—usually reaches the target within 7–9 adjustments, recovers in 1–2 steps after a disturbance, and improves overall quality by 33%+ over common methods.

大模型辅助的工艺参数推荐LLM-assisted Parameter Recommendation

  • 解决的问题：注塑成型调参高度依赖老师傅经验，新人上手慢、试错成本高。The problem: injection-molding tuning relies heavily on veteran experience—newcomers ramp up slowly and trial-and-error is costly.
  • 做法：把领域内 819 篇中英文文献做成本地知识库，让大模型像有经验的工艺工程师一样，根据当前质量与目标的差距给出调参建议并解释原因。The approach: build a local knowledge base from 819 Chinese/English papers so an LLM acts like an experienced process engineer—recommending parameter changes with reasons, based on the gap to target.
  • 效果：在多种材料的仿真任务里，基本 3 轮内就能调到目标质量范围。The result: across multi-material simulation tasks, it reaches the target quality range within about 3 rounds.

多阶段双注意力 LSTM 质量预测（PDA-LSTM）Multi-stage Dual-attention LSTM (PDA-LSTM)

面向注塑这类分多个阶段进行的生产过程的质量预测方法：先把生产过程按工序阶段切分，再用两层注意力机制自动定位对最终质量影响最大的时段和变量，在提升预测精度的同时让预测结果可解释、可追溯。A quality-prediction method for multi-stage processes like injection molding: the process is first split into stages, then two attention layers locate the time windows and variables that matter most to final quality—improving accuracy while keeping predictions interpretable and traceable.

参与contributor

工业加热过程时序预测（TRA-LSTM）Industrial Heating Time-series Forecast (TRA-LSTM)

面向工业加热设备的温度时间序列预测，采用带"再注意力"损失的编码-解码 LSTM，强化模型对长时间跨度温度变化趋势的捕捉，从而提升长时序预测的准确性与稳定性。For temperature time-series of industrial heaters, an encoder–decoder LSTM with a "re-attention" loss strengthens the capture of long-horizon trends, improving accuracy and stability of long-range forecasts.

参与contributor

注塑重量控制：把优化变成分类（SVC + PSO）Weight Control via Classification (SVC + PSO)

把"将产品调到目标重量"这一连续优化问题巧妙地转化为一个分类问题：用支持向量分类（SVC）判断参数是否合格、配合粒子群优化（PSO）搜索，在实验样本很少的情况下也能高效找到达标的工艺参数。Reframes "tune the part to a target weight" from continuous optimization into classification: a support-vector classifier judges whether settings are acceptable while particle-swarm optimization searches—efficient even with very few experimental samples.

参与contributor

注塑工艺参数自学习优化（IGAO）Self-learning Parameter Optimization (IGAO)

一种逐批自学习的工艺优化思路：每生产一批，就用梯度近似估计参数该往哪个方向调、并自适应调整步长，无需精确的物理模型即可一批批逼近目标质量。这也是我后续多目标优化工作的重要思想来源之一。A batch-by-batch self-learning scheme: each batch uses gradient approximation to decide the tuning direction with an adaptive step size, approaching the target without an exact physics model—an important inspiration for my later multi-objective work.

红外热成像内部缺陷检测（DAE-SWnet）Infrared Internal-defect Detection (DAE-SWnet)

Manufacturing Systems）。Automatic internal-defect detection via infrared thermography: pulse-thermography augmentation expands just 2 samples to ~16,000 images, then a denoising-autoencoder + Swin-Transformer segmentation network locates defects unsupervised—74.0% IoU, 97.7% accuracy, 0.28 s/image (published in J. Manufacturing Systems).
