抛射发射机构与弹道动力学建模Launcher Mechanism & Ballistics Modeling

TAM212 动力学课程项目 · 2022 春TAM212 Dynamics course · Spring 2022团队成员Team member抛体运动Projectile motion频闪摄影测量Stroboscopic measurement

动力学课程的设计 + 实验项目：设计一套发射机构，并通过频闪相机实验标定空气阻力系数，用于对抛射体弹道做动力学建模与预测。A dynamics course design + experiment: design a launcher mechanism and calibrate the air-drag coefficient via stroboscopic-camera experiments, then model and predict the projectile's trajectory.

背景Background

课程要求把质点 / 刚体运动学与动力学理论用于真实机构；空气阻力是预测落点的关键不确定量，需用实验标定。The course applies particle/rigid-body kinematics and dynamics to a real mechanism; air drag is the key uncertainty for landing prediction and must be calibrated experimentally.

我的工作What I Did

  • 参与发射机构设计（发射体、限位、触发与击发结构等）。Contributed to the launcher design (emitter, limit stop, trigger/firing parts).
  • 用频闪相机拍乒乓球竖直下落，逐点测速求加速度，反解空气阻力系数。Used a stroboscopic camera on a vertically falling ping-pong ball to measure velocity/acceleration and back-solve the drag coefficient.
  • 分析误差来源（乒乓球过轻、气流扰动、频闪为估计值）。Analyzed error sources (too-light ball, air disturbance, stroboscopic estimates).
  • 将标定的阻力系数代入弹道模型预测发射轨迹。Fed the calibrated coefficient into a ballistics model to predict the trajectory.

成果Results

  • 验证模型预测与实测轨迹的一致性，体现机构设计 + 运动学/动力学建模 + 实验测量的结合。Validated model vs. measured trajectory, combining mechanism design, kinematics/dynamics modeling and experiment.
