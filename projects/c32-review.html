智能注塑成型“感知-优化-控制”技术综述Review: Sensing–Optimization–Control in Intelligent Injection Molding

综述 · 领域知识Review · domain knowledge发表于 Adv. Polymer Technology (2020)Published in Adv. Polymer Tech. (2020)感知-优化-控制Sense–Optimize–Control

系统提出“智能注塑 = 感知 + 优化 + 控制 三位一体”的概念框架，梳理物理量检测、工艺参数优化、机器变量控制三大环节的代表方法与未来方向，是理解该方向的体系化地图。Proposes a unified “intelligent injection molding = sensing + optimization + control” framework, surveying representative methods across physical-quantity sensing, parameter optimization and machine-variable control, plus future directions—a systematic map of the field.

背景Background

注塑约占塑料制品 80%，长期依赖人工经验与试错，质量一致性差。Injection molding makes ~80% of plastic products yet long relied on manual experience and trial-and-error, with poor consistency.

内容主线Structure

  • 感知——温度、压力、熔体状态等相关物理量检测。Sensing — detecting temperature, pressure, melt state and other physical quantities.
  • 优化——工艺参数寻优方法。Optimization — methods for tuning process parameters.
  • 控制——机器变量控制策略。Control — strategies for controlling machine variables.
  • 展望——未来研究方向与值得探索的算法与技术。Outlook — future directions and promising algorithms/techniques.

价值Value

  • 把零散研究归纳成统一框架，便于定位问题与技术选型。Organizes scattered research into one framework for problem-locating and technology selection.
  • 帮助建立对智能制造 / 注塑全链路的体系化认知。Builds a systematic understanding of the full smart-manufacturing / molding pipeline.

说明Note

作为领域知识 / 学习成果（非个人项目）：本人熟悉该“感知-优化-控制”体系，用于建立全局认知与技术选型视野。As domain knowledge (not a personal project): I am familiar with this sense–optimize–control framework, which informs my big-picture view and technology selection.
