关于我About

我的研究主线是回答高端制造中的一个共性难题：如何在实验成本高、参数耦合强、多质量指标冲突的条件下，又快又稳地优化工艺并提前预测质量。在注射成形场景下，我提出了少样本多目标优化方法（7–9 次迭代稳定收敛），构建了仅凭参数即可在成型前预测质量的多模态模型（RMSE 最多降低 36.3%），并探索了知识库增强大模型的参数推荐（3 轮内收敛）。我习惯把方法做到能在仿真平台和真实产线上跑通、能开源、能写成论文——这正是我想在企业研发岗继续做的事。 My work addresses a recurring challenge in high-end manufacturing: how to optimize processes and predict quality quickly and stably when experiments are costly, parameters are coupled, and quality targets conflict. In injection molding I developed a few-shot multi-objective optimizer (stable convergence in 7–9 iterations), a parameter-only pre-cycle multimodal quality-prediction model (up to 36.3% lower RMSE), and explored knowledge-augmented LLMs for parameter recommendation (convergence within 3 rounds). I take methods all the way to running on simulation platforms and real production lines, open-sourcing them, and publishing—exactly what I want to keep doing in an industrial R&D role.

少样本多目标工艺参数优化与生产验证Few-shot Multi-objective Process Optimization with On-line Validation

  • 面向参数多、质量指标相互制约、实验成本高的高端制造，研究不依赖完整机理模型与大规模训练样本的迭代式参数优化方法。An iterative parameter-optimization method that needs neither a full physics model nor large training sets, for high-end manufacturing with many coupled, conflicting quality targets.
  • 基于少量扰动实验与历史批次数据，用二阶近似 + 正则化求解估计各参数对质量的影响方向；按误差阶段动态调整步长与动量，将多目标更新拆分为方向与幅度。Estimates each parameter's effect direction via second-order approximation + regularization from a few perturbation trials and historical batches; adapts step size and momentum by error stage, splitting multi-objective updates into direction and magnitude.
  • 仿真与生产设备验证：7–9 次调整内稳定达标，受扰动后 1–2 次恢复；物理实验整体质量评分较常用梯度优化提升 33%+。Validated in simulation and on production machines: reaches targets within 7–9 adjustments, recovers in 1–2 steps after disturbance; +33% overall quality score vs. common gradient methods on physical trials.

  • 构建参数、传感器时序与质检结果的多模态建模流程，将历史周期组织为可检索的"过程记忆库"，通过阶段划分与特征融合匹配相似历史经验。Built a multimodal pipeline over parameters, sensor time-series and inspection results, organizing past cycles into a retrievable "process memory" matched via stage segmentation and feature fusion.
